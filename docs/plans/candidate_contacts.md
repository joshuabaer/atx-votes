## Research Notes

1. **Contact emails** were difficult to find publicly for most campaigns. Many candidates use contact forms on their websites rather than listing direct email addresses. Visiting each campaign website's contact page directly would yield the best results.

2. **Campaign managers/press contacts** were not publicly listed in search results for the vast majority of campaigns. For press inquiries, the most reliable approach is to use the press email on each campaign's website (where available) or reach out via the general contact form.

3. **Andrew White** dropped out of the governor's race on January 5, 2026 and endorsed Gina Hinojosa, but his name still appears on the ballot.

4. **District discrepancy:** The ballot data listed several HD-49 Democrat candidates under "HD-48." Research confirms they are running in HD-49 (Gina Hinojosa's vacated seat). Donna Howard is the HD-48 incumbent.

5. **CD-37 vs CD-10 discrepancy:** Several candidates listed under CD-37 (Chris Gober, Jessica Karlsruher, Rob Altman, Scott MacLeod) appear to be running in CD-10 based on current Ballotpedia and FEC data. The CD-37 Republican primary has three confirmed candidates: Ge'Nell Gary, Lauren B. Pena, and Janet Malzahn.

6. **Anthony Gupta** (HD-48 Republican) had no publicly findable campaign website or social media presence as of this research date.

7. **Uncontested incumbents skipped:** Greg Abbott (R, Governor), Dan Patrick (R, Lt. Governor), and Greg Casar (D, CD-35) were excluded per instructions.
